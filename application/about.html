Greg

An easy to use calendar for KaiOS

How to use

  • - Key 1 & 3 : Jump between the months
  • - Key \* : Jump to current Day
  • - Key Enter: toggle views
  • - Key 2: event slider in month view
  • - Key #: toggle moon phase view

Nextcloud account

if you want to use an external calendar, you have to enter the correct server address. at nextcloud as follows: - server: "https://xxxxx/remote.php/dav", - username: "xxxxm", - password: "xxxxx",

Features

  • Local Calendar
  • Nextcloud calendar
  • Google calendar
  • subscribe to public calendars
  • moon phases
I tested subscribing to calendar with google calendar. you have the possibility to subscribe to public calendars and private calendars, in the google calendar settings you will find an ID that you can enter in greg (by hand or qr code).

How to install

you can find the app in the bHaCkErSTORe or in the KaiOs Store or you can sideload it.

Privacy Policy

What data are requested ?

Google Account & CalDav Account

  • Information about the individual calendars
  • Calendar events

What data is stored ?

Google Account

If you have connected your Google account to greg, the username and password of your Google account will not be saved on the app, an authentication via oAuth will be done.

The information on the individual calendars and the calendar events is saved locally

CalDAV Account

If you have connected the app to a calDAV account, the username and password are saved locally in the app

The information on the individual calendars and the calendar events is saved locally

Local Calendar

the calendar events are only saved locally on your device.

What data is shared ?

If you have connected the app to a google or calDAV account the requested data, it will be synchronized with the server to always be up to date.


No data analyzes of your data are carried out in the app.

What happens if I delete the app

All your data will be deleted from the device. If you have backed up your calendar data with the app, you must delete it from the device yourself.
Data that is synchronized with a Google account or CalDAV account will not be deleted from the server.
